Our Process

Discovery and Analysis

Project kickoff. Research into the purpose and objectives of the project and understanding our client from the start is key to a successful project.

Design Development

Carry out design work in collaboration with client to gain sign off and proceed with pre-construction government approvals and site preparation.

Construction

Carry out site construction in accordance with the design and quality / time / safety / direction set out in initial project stages.

Post Construction

Carry out time defects while completing testing & commissioning, final approvals and prepare site for client move in.

Post Occupancy

Ongoing inspections and client check in to ensure client satiscation with space and provide ongoing maintenance if required.
